# HTML5 Sampler

Load your custom sounds and play with it directly in your web browser. Supports MIDI controllers 🎹 🎛 ️🎚️

## Support

| Feature | Chrome | Firefox | Safari | Opera | Edge | IE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sampler | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❔ | ❔ | ❔ |
| Load local sounds | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❔ | ❔ | ❔ |
| MIDI Support | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
